About Ca$h for Tra$h

At Ca$h for Tra$h, we believe that waste can be a valuable resource. We provide an easy and rewarding way for individuals and businesses to recycle their waste. By participating in our program, you not only contribute to the environment but also earn money for doing so. We collect various types of waste, including e-waste, plastic, metal, old clothes, and more. Join us in making a positive impact on the planet!
